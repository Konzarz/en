Who is Konzarz?

Hello! My name is Konzarz. My online presence is not just a hobby; it's a long-term project that evolves with my passions and skills. This site is the heart of my activity and your guide to my projects and thoughts.

From YouTube to Broad Horizons

My internet history has its foundation on YouTube. I took my first steps there on July 7, 2019, and I have been running my current channel since June 12, 2020. Although video was the beginning of my journey and I plan to return to publishing tutorials in the future, my current priorities lie elsewhere – in building a brand across multiple levels and creating specific technological solutions.

My Business: KZ Company

Under the brand KZ Company (pol. Firma Konzarz), I carry out projects that require precision and technical knowledge. I don't just observe the digital world; I actively co-create it:

  • Programming and Web Design: I create websites, focusing on their functionality and aesthetics.
  • Game Modifications: I create original add-ons and mods that transform the gaming experience.
  • Tutorials: I share technical knowledge, helping others understand the complexities of digital tools.

Observer and Commentator of Reality

Konzarz is also a place for reflection. My interests include technology, politics, geopolitics, and history. I am not afraid to take on difficult topics, and on my blog, I regularly commemorate important anniversaries and national events, such as November 11. I believe that understanding historical and political processes allows for better navigation in today's world.

Why Konzarz?

Under this name, I combine everything I am interested in. Since 2022, I have been sharing my passion in a more consolidated way, being active on almost every social platform. Every project of mine – from a blog post to advanced website code – is the result of a passion for creation and continuous development.

Konzarz is a synergy of technology, history, and a unique perspective on the world. I invite you to follow my projects under the flag of KZ Company.
